About Me

The NBA Finals are happening in 2019. Kawhi Leonard has almost won the championship, and I hadn't watched a single game of basketball. I now cannot go a day without watching a game. How times have changed! I could never get into football, the action was too far and between. Basketball is a different story; great talent, lots of drama, oh and a lot of scoring! I hope you enjoy my website where I showcase some of my favourite moments, and update you with the latest news in basketball.
